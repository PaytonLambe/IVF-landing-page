ESO Transport Services

Same Day Delivery


We utilize ground transportation to transport bio medical material with a focus on embryos, sperm, and eggs. Our delivery range is primarily throughout North Texas, but further distances and out-of-state deliveries are considered on a case-by-case basis. Our team is available 24/7 and our dry shippers are always charged to ensure short-term and same-day delivery demands can be met.


Dry Shipper Equipment Rental


It is our preference to remain responsible and liable for your precious material by performing 100% of the transportation process. Our vehicles are equipped with a mount for the dry shipper which secures it in its upright position and minimizes any movement throughout transport. Our professional team is bonded and insured and famiiar with the equipment and process. However, we understand that there are those who would prefer to make the delivery themselves. Thereforem, we offer a rental service for our dry shippers.
